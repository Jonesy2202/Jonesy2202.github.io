Wire Models

I started creating models in wire when I was studying art at GCSE where I made a range of insects and decided to carry on with this through my A-levels where I made a large number of birds and other creatures.

My Drawings

I also like to draw in my spare time. I like to draw digitally or just use physical mediums. I have recently tired using alcohol based markers mixed with pencils and normal pens to make my drawings vibrant and detailed.
